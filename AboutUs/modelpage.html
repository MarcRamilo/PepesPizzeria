PEPE'S LEGACY


Pepe's Pizzeria began with the grand opening of the original establishment in 2004, who we all came to love and appreciate. Experience has been passed down from generation to generation; and although Pepe retired, the recipes, service, and experience are still the same! Come on down to Pepe's Pizzeria, we can't wait to see you again!





Pepes Pizzeria

Pepes Pizzeria is a new player in the restaurant industry. The restaurant is in a comfortable, familiar, small town that has a strong need for additional dining options. Bolstered by the need for more choices in family-oriented dining experiences, combined with the option for home-delivery, the restaurant is positioned to take advantage of the market need and serve the families in the Deauville area.
