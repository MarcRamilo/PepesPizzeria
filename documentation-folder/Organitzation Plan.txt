To organize the project, every member will be responsible for their own page on the web. The website will have the following pages:

1. Home --> The landing page with the navbar, banner, logo, button, etc.
2. Menu --> A simple page with an image of the pizzeria's menu, banner, navbar, button.
3. About Us --> A page where we showcase our team, a picture, social media icons, maps location.
4. Our Team --> 3 Individual pages, one for each member with a portrait shot, name, occupation, description and social media.
